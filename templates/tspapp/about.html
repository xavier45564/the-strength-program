About us

Who is The Strength Program?

The Strength Program (TSP) acts as a solutions hub, connecting the community to mental health resources throughout the State of Maryland. Using our services as a Psychiatric Rehabilitation Program, consumers and their families are connected to therapist, behavioral, social, and community support. These connections help establish a positive change in the personal, and professional dynamic of those affected by trauma-informed programming. We equip individuals with the skills to develop positive relationships with themselves, authority figures and their peers which promotes healthy independence. We also offer resources for independent living, and substance use counseling.

Acts as a solutions hub, providing mental health services and building community trust while helping families understand the strength of a powerful mindset. Using our services as a Psychiatric Rehabilitation Program (PRP), clients and their families are educated through behavioral, social, and recreational support that includes group and individual counseling. Clients are also exposed to community resources in mental health, promoting individual success and strengthening social and personal relationships. We offer resources for independent living, practical life skills, interpersonal and social skills, behavioral self-management, substance use counseling, medication management and community adaptation.

Who is Qualified for PRP Services?

Adolescent PRP Qualifications

Active Maryland State Medicaid
Age: 5-17
Axis I diagnosis from a licensed mental health professional
Intake and rehabilitation assessment with TSP staff (parent or legal guardian must be present)
Referral from treating Licensed Mental Health Professional
Who is Qualified for PRP Services?

Adult PRP Qualifications

Intake and Rehabilitation Assessment with PPS staff
Maryland Medicaid and/or proof of application
Psychiatric diagnosis that meets the priority population determined by the Mental Health Authority
Referral from treating Licensed Mental Health Professional
