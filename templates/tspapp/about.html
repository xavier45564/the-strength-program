About us

WHO IS THE STRENGTH PROGRAM

In short terms we are a program that cares about you in long terms we are an program that...

Acts as a solutions hub, providing mental health services and building community trust while helping families understand the strength of a powerful mindset. Using our services as a Psychiatric Rehabilitation Program (PRP), active clients and their families are educated through behavioral, social, and recreational support in group and individual settings; and are exposed to community resources in mental health, with hopes of building community trust, promoting individual success and strengthening the structure of social and personal relationships. We offer resources for independent living, practical life skills, interpersonal and social skills, behavioral self-management, drug abuse counseling, medication management and community adaptation.
